Book Description
The rumor mill of Olympus is constantly churning, but Persephone and Hades are all anyone can talk about. With the constant gossip creating intense pressure on the pair, they decide to slow down their budding romance and focus on sorting out their own issues first.

Lore Olympus: Volume One

Persephone, young goddess of spring, is new to Olympus. Her mother, Demeter, has raised her in the mortal realm, but after Persephone promises to train as a sacred virgin, she's allowed to live in the fast moving, glamorous world of the gods. When her roommate, Artemis, takes her to a party, her entire life changes: she ends up meeting Hades and feels an immediate spark with the charming yet misunderstood ruler of the Underworld. Lore Olympus: Volume Two

Persephone was ready to start a new life when she left the mortal realm for Olympus. However, she quickly discovered the dark side of her glamorous new home from the relatively minor gossip threatening her reputation to a realm shattering violation of her safety by the conceited Apollo and she's struggling to find her footing in the fast-moving realm of the gods. Hades is also off balance, fighting against his burgeoning feelings for the young goddess of spring while maintaining his lonely rule of the Underworld. As the pair are drawn ever closer, they must untangle the twisted webs of their past and present to build toward a new future.

Persephone

  • Persephone, born as Kore is the Goddess of Spring.
  • She was born in the moral realm to Demeter, the Goddess of the Harvest.
  • When she was 19 she moved to Olympus to study biochemistry theory.
  • She is a candidate for The Goddesses of Eternal Maidenhood.
  • She is is intelligent, enjoys school, and is strong willed.

Hades

  • Hades, born Aldoneus is the God of the Dead, King of the Underworld, and is the CEO of Underworld Corp.
  • He is the older brother to Zeus and Poseiden.
  • Most people are afraid of him or avoid him because of his title.
  • He is polite, sensitive, melancholy, and has a temper.

Demeter

  • Demeter is the goddess of harvest and agriculture and the mother of Persephone
  • She currently resides in the Mortal Realm.
  • She is one of the Six Traitors Dynasty that helped over throw the Titans.
  • She is hardworking, strong-willed, protective, obstinate, and headstrong.

Zues

  • Zues, is the God of the Sky and Thunder and is the King of Gods.
  • He is one of the Six Traitors Dynasty that helped over throw the Titans.
  • He is the youngest brother to Hades and Poseidon and the husband of Hera.
  • He is cheeky, stubborn, promiscuous, unscrupulous, dishonest, and egocentric.

Hera

  • Hera is goddess of Marriage and Women, and the Queen of Gods.
  • She is one of the Six Traitors Dynasty that helped over throw the Titans.
  • She is the wife of Zeus, mother to Eris, Ares, Hebe, and Hephaestus.
  • She is strong-willed, empathetic, caring, and has bit of a temper.

Eros

  • Eros is the God of Love.
  • He is one of Persephone's best friends and is always there for her when she's upset.
  • Eros is sweet, caring, empathetic, and mischievous.

Artemis

  • Artemis is the Goddess of the Hunt.
  • She is a member of The Goddesses of Eternal Maidenhood (TGOEM).
  • She is the twin sister to Apollo and is Persephone's roommate and best friend.
  • She is mature, honest, confident, competitive, and genuine.

Apollo

  • Apollo is the God of the Sun, Music, and Medicine.
  • He is the main antagonist in Lore Olympus and has is obsessed with Persephone.
  • He is Artemis's twin brother.
  • He is very confident, arrogant, and possessive.

About Rachel Smythe

Rachel Smythe is #1 New York Times Best Selling Author. She is a Harvey Award winner and Eisner Award winner. Smythe is a comic artist and folklorist, who is best known for her web comic Lore Olympus. She currently lives in Wellington, New Zealand. She likes cartoons, movies, podcasts, folklore, snacks, and coffee. Since 2007 she has been working as a freelance designer and illustrator. In 2018 she started working for Webtoon Entertainment Inc. where she works full time on Lore Olympus.
